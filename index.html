Fashion Designer Kabeer Rajpoot

Kabeer is a Fashion Designer living in Lahare Pakistan. Studied Fine Arts at the Academy of Amsterdam and has been working as an artist, designer and teacher for many years. Specialised in felting very light and delicate shawls, dresses, jackets, vests and shrouds. Design of interior items as cushions, lamps and panels with the idea to bring that beautiful natural feeling into our homes.
